## 日本語訳

データガバナンスとセキュリティプラットフォームを提供するQueryPieは、ドキュメントコラボレーションツール「Typed」と提携し、社内外の業務における生産性と協業効率の向上を進めています。

複数の顧客やチームと並行して業務を進めるQueryPieでは、コンテンツ管理、技術文書、レポート、ワークフロー、計画資料を一元管理できる強力なワークマネジメント基盤が必要でした。Typedは、リサーチとアウトプットを一つの場所でつなぐことで、単一の信頼できる情報源を構築し、チーム間連携をよりスムーズにする役割を担います。

記事では、Spaces、Inbox、Projects、Document、Library、Knowledge Network、Tasks、Highlights、Review Requestsなどの主要機能が紹介されており、情報整理・参照・レビュー依頼までを一つの流れで支援できる点が強調されています。

QueryPieのCISOであるKenny Park氏は、Typedの導入によってG Suite上で分散していたリンクや参考資料を自動的に整理できるようになり、ドキュメント管理の負担が減り、ビジネスチーム全体の生産性と協業性が高まったと述べています。

## 原文（英語）

**Original title:** Data governance and security solutions provider “QueryPie” partners with collaborative documentation tool “Typed” to enhance productivity and streamline collaboration

Korea’s fast-growing data governance and security platform QueryPie has partnered with Typed to enhance productivity and streamline collaboration across their processes. Typed is also a member of the Born2Global Centre.

Working with multiple clients and teams across the world, QueryPie was in need of a robust work management tool for content management, technical documentation, reporting, workflows, and planning that could ensure a single source of truth for the entire content repository, in addition to a more streamlined process for team collaboration. This led QueryPie to partner with Typed, a document-based collaboration tool designed to boost productivity by seamlessly integrating research and output in one place.

Focused on an information-rich experience, Typed’s knowledge management features ensure that all links, tasks, resources, and videos are collated together in one place while creating content.

Typed’s major features include Spaces for organizing work by business need, an Inbox for uncategorized resources, Projects for grouping documents by initiative, Documents that extend the familiar G Suite workflow, a Library for collecting webpages, PDFs, images, videos, and memos, a Knowledge Network that visualizes relationships among documents, Tasks with deadlines and Google Calendar synchronization, Highlights for key text, and Review Requests for cross-team collaboration.

Typed Founder and CEO Woojin Kim said, “QueryPie’s partnership with Typed will help streamline their business workflow, making documents quicker, smarter, and more powerful. Our ultimate goal is to provide a solution that saves QueryPie time and increases their team’s productivity so that they can focus on supplying robust data governance solutions to their clients.”

QueryPie’s CISO, Kenny Park, added, “Typed has changed the way we approach our documentation. While working in G Suite, we never realized how unproductive and tiring it can be to juggle multiple websites, links, resources, and research materials. Typed provides what you need automatically, which allows our team to focus better. Also, with hundreds of documents and no native document management structure in G Suite, an organized folder structure is exactly what we needed. Our business team is already more productive and collaborative.”

## About Typed

Typed, developed by Business Canvas, is a collaborative documentation tool that optimizes teamwork by connecting scattered documents and workflows. Its core technology rethinks information management by capturing relational data among files and syncing them with user behavior, giving teams an environment where information can be efficiently reused and collaboration streamlined.

## About QueryPie

QueryPie is an advanced data governance and service organization control solution developed by CHEQUER that simplifies data access and manages scattered data sources and data protection policies. QueryPie provides visibility and control over cloud applications, data, and users across multiple databases and cloud data platforms, helping enterprises maintain privacy and comply with regulations such as ISMS, PCI-DSS, GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA.
