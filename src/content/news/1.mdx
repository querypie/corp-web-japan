# QueryPie、韓国でOkta Integration Networkパートナーに認定

> Source article: [CIO Applications](https://robotic-process-automation.cioapplications.com/news/querypie-becomes-okta-integration-network-partner-in-korea-nid-8103.html)

## 日本語訳

QueryPieは韓国で初めてOktaとの正式な連携認定を受け、Okta Integration Network（OIN）を通じて利用可能になりました。これにより、企業はOktaの認証基盤とQueryPieのデータガバナンス機能をよりシームレスに組み合わせられるようになります。

Oktaのアジア太平洋地域アライアンス担当ディレクターであるMatthew Paull氏は、Oktaのパートナーネットワーク拡大の流れの中で、QueryPieとの提携によって地域企業のデジタルワークフロー簡素化を後押しできると述べています。

OINはSaaSアプリケーション連携を通じて第三者サービスへの安全なアクセスを提供するパートナーネットワークです。QueryPieはこのエコシステムを通じてSAMLベースの認証を提供し、企業がOktaのアカウント管理基盤を活用しながらQueryPieへ容易にアクセスできるようにします。

Brant Hwang氏は、データソースが分散する現代の企業環境では、所有者・利用状況・データ移動の追跡が難しいと指摘し、Oktaとの連携を通じて既存環境に無理なく組み込めるアクセス制御とプライバシー保護ソリューションを提供していく考えを示しました。

## 原文（英語）

**Original title:** QueryPie becomes Okta Integration Network Partner in Korea

QueryPie is a powerful data governance solution developed by a team of database specialists at CHEQUER that makes it simple to manage disparate data sources and security standards in one location.

**FREMONT, CA:** For the first time in Korea, QueryPie, the data governance platform, has been verified to operate with Okta and is now available through the Okta Integration Network (OIN). QueryPie plans to promote more successful client use cases in the future by facilitating technical alliances in addition to its association with Okta's OIN Network.

"As Okta increases momentum with its partner network across Asia, we are thrilled to partner with QueryPie and extend our reach locally. Together with QueryPie as part of Okta Integration Network, businesses will be able to seamlessly tap into Okta's tools for simplifying the digital workflow," said Matthew Paull, Okta's Director of Regional Alliances for the Asia Pacific.

The Okta Integration Network is an Okta partner network that provides secure access to third-party services through SaaS application integrations. Through Okta's partner ecosystem, QueryPie enables SAML-based authentication. Organizations can easily access QueryPie's services thanks to Okta's account management service.

QueryPie is a powerful data governance solution developed by a team of database specialists at CHEQUER that makes it simple to manage disparate data sources and security standards in one location. QueryPie delivers data governance within the business through a web-based solution by giving user-specific data access management and auditing. Companies like Kakao and Yanolja used QueryPie to improve security policies and comply with laws like PCI-DSS in response to the increasing rules and compliance on data.

"We're thrilled to become a part of the Okta Integration Network," said Brant Hwang, CEO of CHEQUER. "Today, a lot of data teams struggle to manage data since data sources are scattered everywhere, which makes it hard to follow up on who owns and uses them, where data goes, and how they're being used. We offer streamlined access control and privacy solutions that seamlessly integrate with existing data environments, including Okta's identity provider system. We are currently working to leverage this opportunity to broaden QueryPie's user base in Korea to include both startups and enterprises and produce tangible results for both platforms by connecting Okta to clients interested in simplifying their digital transformation workflow — identity provider service or data governance."

Okta is the most well-known independent identity management company. Organizations may use the Okta Identity Cloud to link the right people to the right technology at the right moment. Okta delivers simple and secure access to users and organizations worldwide, with over 7,000 pre-built connectors to apps and infrastructure providers, giving them the confidence to fulfill their full potential.
